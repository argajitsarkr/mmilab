From Local Insight to Global Impact

We're combating antimicrobial resistance through phytochemical–antibiotic synergies, AI-driven drug discovery, and phage-based therapeutics at Tripura University.

Explore Research

The MMI Lab at the Department of Molecular Biology & Bioinformatics, Tripura University (A Central University), investigates antimicrobial resistance through combination therapy with phytochemicals, AI-based drug discovery and repurposing, and the impact of heavy metals on bacterial resistance.

Our interdisciplinary team integrates microbiology, artificial intelligence, and phage biology to develop next-generation strategies against WHO-priority pathogens.
