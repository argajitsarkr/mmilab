Principal Investigator

Dr. Surajit Bhattacharjee, PhD

Associate Professor

Department of Molecular Biology & Bioinformatics

Tripura University (A Central University), Suryamaninagar, Tripura, India

PhD in Microbiology and Immunology (Bose Institute, Jadavpur University) with 15+ years of research and teaching experience. Former Postdoctoral Research Associate at the University of Illinois at Chicago, USA.

Research expertise in Leishmania and bacterial biofilm-associated infection, animal models of Leishmanial infection, cell signaling, immune response during host-pathogen interaction, and antimicrobial drug discovery. Recipient of six extramural research grants as PI from DBT, DST-SERB, ICMR, and BIRAC.
